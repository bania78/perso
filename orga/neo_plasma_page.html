Introduction:

Neo Team Plasma is a criminal organization in Unys. Following the events of Pokémon Black and White, Team Plasma split into two separate groups. The first has remained loyal to N, and wishes to repair their past mistakes, while the second, Neo Team Plasma has come under the command of Ghetis, and wishes to take control of Unova by force. The two groups have become enemies, and do not hesitate to attack the other.
