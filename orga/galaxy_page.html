Introduction:

Team Galactic is a former criminal organization that works today in the search for a new energy in Sinnoh. in the old days, their goal is not limited to the world, but is the recreation of the entire universe. Led by its leader and founder Helio, it wished to obtain control of the two primordial Pokémon Dialga and Palkia in order to alter the dimensions of time and space, with the aim of creating a new "purified" universe. In some aspects, both in terms of purpose and methods, the behavior of Team Galaxie could recall that of a sect. It is now led by Saturn, a former commander who uses his influence to bring Galactic Team back on track.
