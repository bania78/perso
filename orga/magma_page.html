Introduction:

Team Magma is one of two former criminal organization in Hoenn. Their goal is to create a world for human progress by making Groudon undergo Primal Reversion to expand the landmass to give humanity more space on which to build and develop civilization. Unlike Team Rocket, the members of Team Magma seem more guided by blind fanaticism and a certain lack of awareness of the consequences of their actions than by a real desire to harm. Their costume is reminiscent of desert thieves or devils.
