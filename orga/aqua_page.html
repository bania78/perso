Introduction:

Team Aqua is one of two former criminal organization in Hoenn. Today it protects Saliria the underwater city, prey to many pirates for its riches and its rare Pokémon. In the old days, they are trying to return the world to a state for Pokémon by awakening Kyogre to wipe out human civilization and return the world to its beginnings, so that Pokémon can live untainted by humanity's progress. Unlike Team Rocket, the members of Team Aqua seem more guided by blind fanaticism and a certain lack of awareness of the consequences of their actions than by a real will to harm. Their costume is reminiscent of pirates.
