Introduction:

Team Plasma is a former criminal organization in Unys. They wish to repair their past mistakes and take care of the Pokémon abandoned by their trainer. Before that, Team Plasma's original goal was to "liberate" all Pokémon from their Trainers, by either manipulating the Trainer to release their Pokémon, or by theft. Ghetsis, Team Plasma's true leader, exploited the group in an attempt to rule Unys as the only one with Pokémon.
