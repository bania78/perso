Introduction:

Rocket Team is a criminal organization whose goal is world domination by all possible criminal means (theft, burglary, kidnapping, racketeering, etc.). They are based in the Kanto and Johto regions, with a small outpost in the Sevii Islands and in the Orange Archipelago. "Steal Pokémon for profit. Exploit Pokémon for profit. All Pokémon exist for the glory of Team Rocket. The organization, led by its charismatic leader Giovanni (nicknamed "the Boss"), has 3 Branch leaders (one for Kanto, one for Johto and one for the Sevii Islands and in the Orange Archipelago), 4 Commanders and countless of members."

Rocket Team in the Story:

Pokemon Origins' Arc:

In this arc, the Rocket Team conducted by Giovanni will try to build an empire based on pokemon trading and will even try to seize the prototype of the master ball by attacking the Sylph SARL in order to capture powerful pokemon more easily. She will be stopped by Red at the Sylph SARL in Safrania and Giovanni will be defeated in his Jadielle arena by Red as well. Following his defeat Giovanni will dissolve the Rocket Team.

Between Pokemon Origins' Arc and Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

During this period, the commanders reform Team Rocket in the shadows and attempt to find Giovanni.

Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

During this arc, the reformed Team Rocket establishes itself in Johto and will attempt to reconnect with their Boss by taking the Doublonville radio tower and broadcasting a radio message, but this will be routed by Gold. The organization will then be dissolved again by Amos.
