Introduction:

Red is a great Pokemon Trainer of Kanto region from Pallet Town. He is the main player in Rocket Team's first defeat, he was the master of Indigo League before giving way to Peter to continue his journey and he is actually the second trainer in IFPT Ranking.

Red is a young men calm and not very talkative. He is however very affectionate with his pokemon.

Story:

Pokemon Origins' Arc:

During this arc, Red start his journey at the same time as Blue, his rival, after choice his Salamèche, in Chen Professor's laboratory, and loose his first fight against Blue. During his journey through Kanto, Red faces the gym leaders of the region and defeats the Rocket Team which will be dismantled thereafter for the first time following this failure. He then succeeded in defeating the Indigo Elite Four and then becomes Indigo Master after defeat his rival Blue.

Between Pokemon Origins' Arc and Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

One year after his success in Indigo League, Red gives up his title to continue his journey. For a year, he travels through the Johto region and win against the champions of this one. After that, he will go to the Silver Mountain to train.

Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

During this arc, Red confronts Gold at the top of Silver Montain and loose this legendary fight. After that, he decided to start a new journey at Hoenn to progress.
