Introduction:

Blue is the Gym Leader of Viridian City in Kanto from Pallet Town, the grandson of Chen Professor and the rival of Red.

Blue is a fierce competitor whose ambition to become the best Trainer in the world puts everything else in the background. However, he knows how to recognize the strength of an adversary.

Story:

Pokemon Origins' Arc:

During this arc, Blue start his journey at the same time as Red, his rival, after choice his Carapuce, in Chen Professor's laboratory, and win his first fight against Red. During his journey through Kanto, Blue faces the gym leaders of the region, win against the Indigo Elite Four and his Master and then becomes Indigo Master before loose few hours later against Red.

Between Pokemon Origins' Arc and Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

After loose his fight against Red, Blue go to Johto to train. During his journey through Johto Blue defeat all the Gym Leader of the region. After one year of travel in Johto he returns in Kanto and take on the role of Gym Leader in Jadielle.

Pokemon HeartGold/SoulSilver's Arc:

In this arc, Blue loose against Gold in Jadielle's Gym and told him to go find Red a the top of Silver Mountain because he believes that Gold has the level to face him.
