Introduction:

Julie is the best Pokémon trainer in the world today. She comes from the Sinnoh region and was born in Alamos. She is known worldwide for winning the first solo world championship organized by the IFPT by defeating Red in the final.

Julie is a very solitary young woman, sure of herself and very mocking. She is, however, considerate with her Pokémon and the few people she likes.
