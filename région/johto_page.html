Description:

Johto is located west of Kanto, wich together form a joint landmass that is south of Sinnoh. It is a seaside region surrounded by mountain ranges. It has an island, to the west, where Irisia and the Parc Safari are located. Johto is also located northeast of Hoenn. Johto has only one metropolis: Doublonville. There are, among other things, a shopping center, a radio station and the Magnetic Train station. Smaller towns are also found in Johto. For example, Oliville is famous for its port, Irisia for its pharmacy and Mauville for its Tour Chétiflor.

Cities:

New Bark Town:

A town where the wind blows and tells of impending change. This town is located in southeastern Johto and being connected to Route 29 in the west, it is also the town that must be passed through to reach Tohjo Falls, Victory Road, and Indigo Plateau, via Route 26 and Route 27.

Cherrygrove City:

A city where you can smell small flowers and a sea breeze. This city is located in south-central Johto. Though the city has no Gym, it is frequently passed through by Pokémon Trainers on their way to Violet City from New Bark Town.
