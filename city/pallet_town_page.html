Pallet Town

Region

Kanto

Population

168

Description:

A fairly new and quiet town. It's a small and pretty place. The town has two exits. To the north is Route 1, leading to Viridian City. To the south is Route 21, a water route leading to Cinnabar Island.

Location:

Places of interest:

Professor Chen's Lab:

In the southeast corner of town is Professor Oak's Lab. Here, Pokémon Trainers just beginning their quest are given the choice to receive either a Bulbasaur, Charmander, or Squirtle from Professor Oak. On the occasion that Professor Oak is out of the previously listed Pokémon, he has been seen to hand out Pikachu and Eevee.

Red's House:

Actually Red's mother leave alone in the house, she is worried about Red, but also how proud she is of him.

Blue's House:

Actually Nina Chen, Blue's sister, leave here.
